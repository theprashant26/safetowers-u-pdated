WHO WE ARE

We, Safe Towers Private Limited , an ISO 9001:2015 and OHSAS 18001:2007 certified Company has established ourselves as one of the reputed organization engaged in Designing, Manufacturing and Providing Services for Communication and Transmission Line Towers, Radar Navigation Towers, Guyed Towers, Monopoles, Media Display Board, Lightning Protection and Earthing, Solar Photovoltaic, Power Solutions, Shelters, Self Supporting Towers and Mobile Towers. Our services are also sought for Soil Investigation and Foundation Design, RF Survey, LOS Survey, and Tower Erection. We, Safe Towers, has set up quality communication towers which are closest to perfection as for as the quality standards and the specifications of the clients are concerned. We also work on a timed schedule, which is planned well, in order to ensure on-time delivery of our product.

We make effective use of the latest technology in our manufacturing process, in order to set up quality towers. These components are manufactured to satisfy the diverse needs of our clients. The range of choices that we offer, help our clients to make their choice and select the type of tower that would suit their need for their business.

Our team consisting of skilled staff, who do every task related to the setting up of these towers. Their united efforts have enabled us to create an unbeatable position in this highly competitive market.

 
 

COMPANY MISSION

To provide competent structural and civil engineering expertise at a reasonable cost, and to provide a clear and concise product that will work for our esteemed Customers project a success.

To build and deliver turnkey, high-quality and cost-effective communication telecom structural Towers on schedule as a one-stop shop for our Clients.

Continue to focus on the market and introduce a more efficient and innovative product for a longer duration of existence.

 
 

COMPANY VISION

To produce world-class quality products ensuring State-of-the-art Infrastructure and making India the preferred sourcing hub for Global Communication Tower needs.

Founder & Managing Director

He has having more than a decade experience in structural designing field and having 3000 structure design under his belt and well known for his innovative design for any challenged requirement.
